Chicken & Rice

Ingredients: Chicken, panko crumbs, rice, salt, pepper, garlic salt.

Instructions: Cook rice to desired texture. Mix seasonings into panko crumbs and bread chicken. Fry until 165F internal or bake at 400F for 25 minutes.

Spaghetti

Ingredients: Spaghetti noodles, marinara sauce, and beef.

Instructions: Boil spaghetti until tender while browning beef in a seperate pan. Drain grease and add marinara. Drain noodles and serve with beef mixture.

Tacos

Ingredients: Ground beef, taco seasoning, tortillas, lettuce, cheese, salsa.

Instructions: Brown beef and drain grease. Add taco seasoning and water, and let simmer. Serve with desired toppings.

Fajitas

Ingredients: Tortillas, bell peppers, onions, chicken, steak, cheese, sour cream and guac.

Instructions: Cut peppers, onions, steak and chicken into strips and cook in a skillet with desired seasonings. Serve in tortillas with desired toppings.

Tatertot Casserole

Ingredients: Tatertots, ground beef, sour cream, cheddar cheese, cream of mushroom soup.

Instructions: Brown ground beef and drain the grease. Add sour cream and cream of mushroom soup to beef. Layer cheese and tatertots on top of beef mixture in a 9x9 pan. Bake at 375F for 30 minutes.

Cheeseburgers

Ingredients: Ground beef, burger buns, and cheese.

Instructions: Mold beed into patty shape and brown until desired doneness. Flip to other side and place cheese on top to melt. Place on bun and add desired toppings.

Pizza

Ingredients: Pizza dough, cheese, marinara, and pepperoni.

Instructions: Stretch dough to desired size. Spread marinara evenly across the the top of the dough and cover with cheese. Add pepperoni and desired toppings to the pizza. Bake at 375F for 30 minutes.

Steak Quesadilla

Ingredients: Flank stake, jack cheese, flour tortillas, and butter.

Instructions: Sear steak to desired doneness. Butter one side of each tortilla and sandwich cheese and steak inbetween with butter sides on the outside. Let each side cook for several minutes.

Beef & Cabbage

Ingredients: Ground beef, cabbage, salt, pepper, garlic salt, and mayploy.

Instructions: Brown ground beef and drain the grease. Add cabbage and cook until it's soft. Serve with mayploy.

Butternut Squash Soup

Ingredients: Butternut squash, heavy cream, vegetable broth, olive oil.

Instructions: Roast squash at 400F for 30 minutes. Blend roasted squash with broth, oil, and cream. top with more cream and seasonings.

Kebobs

Ingredients: Chicken, steak, and various vegetables.

Instructions: Layer raw chicken, steak, and vegetables of your choice onto a scewer. Season how you like and cook on grill.

French Toast

Ingredients: Sandwich bread, egg, milk, cinnimon, syrup, whipped cream, and berries.

Instructions: Mix eggs, milk, and cinnimon in a container. Dip a piece of bread in the mixure, coving both sides. Place on griddle until cooked. Top with butter, whipped cream, and berries.
